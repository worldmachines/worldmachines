# Wiki

Shared, structured knowledge base for World Machines.

Wiki entries are normally created or updated by AI-generated pull requests that synthesize material from `raw-notes/`. Human maintainers/admins review and approve changes before merge.

## Entry guidelines

- Link back to the raw notes or source materials that motivated the entry.
- Prefer small, focused files over large omnibus pages.
- Distinguish claims, interpretations, open questions, and source quotations.
- Preserve alternate framings when participants use different vocabularies for nearby ideas.

## Suggested structure

Future subdirectories may include:

- `concepts/` — glossary and conceptual ontology.
- `people/` — thinkers, participants, historical actors.
- `texts/` — books, essays, archives, resources.
- `threads/` — cross-cutting themes and live research programs.
- `meetings/` — summaries and follow-ups.
