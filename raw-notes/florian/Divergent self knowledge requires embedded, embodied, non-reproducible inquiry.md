# Divergent self knowledge requires embedded, embodied, non-reproducible inquiry

## Claim
> Humboldt's fieldstudies model a timely mode of self-knowledge under divergence: (1) embed in the matter rather than speculate from the armchair; (2) learn through the body, in the territory, non-reproducibly; (3) shift registers to mark gaps instead of forcing false closure. Not a doctrine — a direction.

## Body
The constructive answer to [[The lab-science toolkit of self-knowledge fails once its preconditions withdraw]].
LIVE TENSION — do not reconcile — with [[Frameworks can turn performative as they become more sophisticated]]: announcing this as "the new method" may itself be another shitty castle. Held as a branch.
Adjacent: [[The margin provides sharper insight on historic infrastructure than the canonical center]]; [[Divergence grows in peripheral fields, not in the fortified center]].

## What would weaken this / change my mind
- if learning about oneself in divergence conditions would be possible via the opposite (distanced and abstract lab-style experiments) 
- If embedded, embodied, non-reproducible inquiry would not lead to useful self knowledge, but causing to get stuck in localized rabbit holes
