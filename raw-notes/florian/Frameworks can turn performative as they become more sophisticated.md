# Frameworks can turn performative as they become more sophisticated

## Claim

> Sophisticated frameworks can replicate the same passive deferral structure they are supposed to help overcome — the apodictical pattern in which any evidence confirms the thesis migrates across intellectual traditions regardless of surface sophistication

## Body

Three instances: 
1) Pangloss/Leibniz (defers to God’s plan)
2) Horto/contemporary tech optimism (defers to Progress’s trajectory)
3) potentially the divergence thesis itself (defers to historical machines — any present dysfunction “explained” by the periodization)

The self-reflexive sting: the WM framework may exhibit the structure it diagnoses. In live tension with [[Canonic infrastructure decays in hilarious drainage]] and the WM apparatus generally — when a theory cannot be falsified by any evidence, it may itself be a performative moat. 

Hold both as a branch; do not reconcile.

## What would weaken this / change my mind
- if this pattern would not generalize from me to others, if others would never struggle with performative framework building
- If the level of framework sophistication would be irrelevant for success rate of an intervention and some other factors would be more influential
