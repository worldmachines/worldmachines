# Zach Adams

**Zach Adams** is the co-creator of [[Dwarf Fortress]] and co-founder of Bay 12 Games, working alongside his brother [[Tarn Adams]].

## Role in Dwarf Fortress

While [[Tarn Adams]] handles virtually all the programming, Zach manages community engagement, business operations, and the broader non-technical aspects of Bay 12 Games. The brothers began making games together as children; DF is the culmination of this lifelong collaboration.

## Significance

Zach represents the often-invisible support infrastructure behind long-term creative projects — not the primary author, but essential to the conditions that made authorship possible. Without the operational and community scaffolding he provides, Tarn's 20-year programming commitment would have been much harder to sustain.
