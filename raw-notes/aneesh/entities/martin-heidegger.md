# Martin Heidegger

## Biographical

German philosopher (1889–1976). Major works: *Being and Time* (1927), *Poetry, Language, Thought* (1971). Associated with phenomenology, existential philosophy, and later philosophy of technology.

Controversial figure due to his involvement with National Socialism; the relationship between his politics and his philosophy remains actively debated.

## Relevant Work

### *Building Dwelling Thinking* (1951, collected in *Poetry, Language, Thought*, 1971)

A lecture/essay exploring the relationship between human existence and space. Key claims:
- Dwelling is the fundamental mode of human being-in-the-world
- Building and thinking must each be subordinated to dwelling
- "To free" means to spare — to preserve something in its own nature
- Boundaries are generative ("that from which something begins its presencing"), not merely limiting
