# Stephen Fry

English actor, novelist, comedian, and public intellectual. Fry has been publicly skeptical of certain AI applications, particularly around creativity and the replacement of human intellectual labor.

## Role in This Corpus

Appears in [[On Being Good vs. Knowing Good]] as the narrator of [[Nick Cave]]'s letter about AI creativity. The specific framing — Fry reading Cave's argument — carries rhetorical significance: two cultural figures with no AI industry involvement, articulating the taste/experience argument that Sathe (as an AI practitioner) finds unexpectedly convincing.

## Broader Intellectual Presence

Fry is known for his engagement with literature, philosophy, and technology — and for a writing style that makes complex ideas accessible. His public profile means that when he narrates an argument about AI's limits, it reaches audiences far beyond the AI research community.
