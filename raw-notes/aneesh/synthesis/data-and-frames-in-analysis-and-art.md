# Data is Always Constructed: From Sensemaking to Art

## The Core Claim

[[Data-Frame Theory of Sensemaking|Klein et al]] make a radical epistemological claim:

> "Data is always constructed — you have to *choose* to interpret certain facts and events as data."

A frame determines what counts as data in the first place. Without a frame, you don't see more — you see nothing coherently. The analyst who "misses" a pattern in a dashboard isn't failing to observe; they are observing without the frame that would make the observation meaningful.

## Resonances Across Domains

### Intelligence Analysis
The paradigm case: the same raw intelligence feed produces completely different outputs depending on the frame the analyst brings. This is why "objectivity" in analysis is a misleading ideal — the question is never whether you have a frame, but whether your frame is accurate and well-updated.

Decision support implications (Klein): systems should track *anchors* (the 3–4 data points that constituted a frame), not maximize data throughput. The analyst drowning in data is worse off than the analyst with a clear frame and selective attention.

### Scientific Observation
Kuhn's paradigm-dependence of observation: anomalies are invisible within a successful paradigm. They only become data once a competing framework makes them relevant. This is the same structural claim as the Data-Frame theory applied to the level of scientific communities rather than individual analysts.

### Artistic Creation and the Literary Canon
Bloom's [[Western Canon]] argument has the same structure: the significance of a literary work is not intrinsic — it is constituted by the frame of literary tradition, influence, and readerly practice. Works "enter" or "exit" the canon not because of fixed properties but because the frames of readers and writers change.

Even more specifically: [[Creative Misreading]] is the mechanism by which a writer constructs a new frame for reading a precursor, which then makes previously invisible features of the precursor's work visible as significant.

### Technology Adoption
Chin's [[How Experts Sensemake]] example: an exec notices a customer-retention anomaly that their lead data analyst sees only as noise. The analyst has the data; they lack the frame. When the exec provides the frame ("this might be a broken onboarding flow"), the "data" becomes visible.

Applied to AI: the field reports that indicate autonomous AI coding is possible are *already available* — they're in public view. But practitioners without a frame that makes "AI without human code review" even hypothetically possible will not register these reports as significant data.

## The Constructivist Implication

This convergence suggests a broad epistemological claim: **structured knowledge (expertise, canon, paradigm) is not just a filter on data — it constitutes what data is available.** The price of having no framework is not neutrality but blindness.

The practical upshot varies by domain:
- **Analysis**: build better frames; track anchors; invest in alternative frame construction
- **Artistic creation**: take on precursors actively; the anxiety is productive, not pathological
- **Technology navigation**: maintain alternative frames in parallel with operating frames; collect fragment libraries

## What's Missing

The claim "data is always constructed" has a dark side: sophisticated practitioners can construct away legitimate anomalies indefinitely. The "preservation cycle" (dismissing incongruent data) can be epistemically virtuous (noise really is noise) or pathological (epistemic closure). The theory doesn't give a principled criterion for distinguishing the two in advance — only retrospectively from outcomes.

## Essay Direction

**Seed**: "The Frame Problem: On Seeing and Not Seeing in Analysis, Science, and Art"

Thesis: across intelligence analysis, scientific inquiry, and artistic creation, the same epistemological structure appears — what you can see depends on the frame you bring. This is not a limitation to overcome but a condition to work with. The expert move in each domain is to develop better frames and cultivate the discipline to notice when the current frame stops working.
