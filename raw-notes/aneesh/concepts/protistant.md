# Protistant

**Coined by**: [[Aneesh Sathe]], [[Not My Lever — The Last Container]] (Section III), 2026.

## Definition

A **protistant** is the figure of the modern individual who must *bound a region of the world themselves* in order to act on it. The word is a portmanteau of *protist* (the single-celled eukaryote that survives in open ocean by maintaining a membrane around a small interior chemistry) and the formal suffix that turns the description into an identity. The implicit echo of *Protestant* is intentional: as the medieval Catholic could rely on the Church to mediate the cosmos, the modern person can rely on no inherited container — they must draw the bounds themselves, alone, the way a Protestant once was made to read scripture without intermediation.

The protistant is not a personality type but a structural position. Anyone living under the conditions of receded containers and an expanded surface of concern is a protistant whether they have noticed it or not.

> "Dispersal across everything produces effective action on nothing. To exercise your intents then needs a new survival measure. Like a protist enclosing a bit of ocean in a membrane, you must enclose a bit of the world. You are a *protistant* now." — Sathe, 2026.

## The Biological Analogy

A protist is a useful model because it is the simplest organism for which the **membrane is the central engineering problem**. The protist does not control the ocean; it controls *what crosses the membrane*. Its interior chemistry is non-trivially different from the surrounding sea, and that difference is the protist's life. Lose the membrane and the protist dissolves into the ocean.

By analogy, the protistant does not control the surface of concern — the algorithm, the climate, the discourse, the war in the feed. The protistant controls *what crosses the membrane they draw*. The interior is a bounded region of the world they have chosen to act on: a project, a craft, a community, a habitat — the [[The Thicket|thicket]] in its concrete form. The exterior remains the full surface; the membrane is what makes intent possible at all.

## Why the Protistant Move Is Required

The protistant move is forced by the conjunction of two long-running historical processes:

1. **[[receding-containers|Containers have receded]].** No perma-framework is available to draw the bounds for you. The Catholic Church drew the bounds for the medieval peasant; the nation drew the bounds for the modern citizen; postmodern pluralism makes both unavailable as load-bearing structures.
2. **[[surface-of-concern|The surface of concern has expanded]] by roughly five orders of magnitude** (the central case of [[the-lightening-of-intent]]). The medieval peasant's surface was the parish. Marcus's was the empire. Yours is the planet, the species, the substrate, the next century, the algorithm, the war that is now in your feed because someone with a phone is in it.

Without an inherited container and against an expanded surface, the only way to exercise intent at all is to draw the bounds yourself. To not draw them is to disperse into the ocean — to be, in the analogy, a region of seawater rather than a protist.

## What the Protistant Does

The protistant's work has three operational moves, each named elsewhere in the canon:

1. **[[not-my-lever|Lever discipline]]** — auditing which causal channels are inside the membrane and refusing the rest with the sentence *"not my lever."* The membrane is drawn by what is named as a lever and what is not.
2. **Inhabit a [[The Thicket|thicket]]** — the concrete shape of the interior chemistry. The thicket is the bit of the world the membrane encloses: a way of being held open from extraction, with feedback short enough that the lever-pulls actually land.
3. **Hold a [[designed-mindset|designed mindset]]** — a module called up for the duration of one project, holding together the bounded effort. Stoic indifference for finishing; Romantic immersion for seeing fresh.

## What the Protistant Is Not

The protistant is not a hermit, an opt-out artist, or a defender of the small for its own sake. The protistant *acts* — the membrane exists so that intent can be exercised, not so that intent can be avoided. The dark mirror of the protistant move is the [[dark-forestry|dark-forestry]] of the self: the membrane drawn to *vacate* responsibility rather than to *create* space for action. From outside the two are indistinguishable; from inside one builds and one consumes.

## The Etymological Tension

The protistant carries two old echoes:

- **Protist (Greek *protistos*, "the very first")**: the earliest eukaryotic life, the move from open chemistry to bounded chemistry. Life's foundational structural move.
- **Protestant**: the move to mediate the cosmos personally rather than through an inherited institution. Modernity's foundational individuating move.

The coinage sits at the intersection: the modern person's response to receded containers is *structurally* the move life made when it first put a membrane between itself and the ocean — and *historically* the move the modern individual has been making since the Reformation, now generalised from theology to intent.
