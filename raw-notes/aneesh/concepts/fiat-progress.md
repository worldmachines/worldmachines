# Fiat Progress

**Coined by**: [[venkatesh-rao|Venkatesh Rao]]
**Primary sources**: [[the-end-of-identity-ai-plasticity-divergence-machine|The End of Identity: AI, Plasticity, and the Divergence Machine]] (Sathe, 2026-03-08); [[venkatesh-rao-the-divergence-machine-ii|Divergence Machine II]] (Rao, Feb 2026)

## Definition

**Fiat Progress** is the [[modernity-machine|Modernity Machine]]'s dominant understanding of Progress. It emerged in the 1200s and was institutionalized worldwide by 1600.

It is "an uncontroversial and relatively naive notion of increasing material well-being yoked to fiat idealism, handed down by religious leaders and the monarchs who patronized them."

The word "fiat" is precise: this is progress by *declaration*, not by demonstration — by decree rather than by earned demonstration. To participate in Fiat Progress was to participate in an essentially religious story, underwritten by the state. Bad times = punishments for bad behavior. Good times = rewards for good behavior.

## Structure: The y=f(x) Framework

In Rao's y=f(x) framework:
- **x** is treated as constant or slowly changing (received tradition defines the scope of relevant reality)
- **f** is a received interpretive tradition (not constructed but inherited)
- **y** can only be sacred or profane according to existing ontology — the question of whether new data is "good" or "bad" is answerable from within the existing framework

Contrast with the [[argument-of-progress|Argument of Progress]], where x expands rapidly within lifetimes, requiring the construction of *new* interpretive frameworks (f) rather than merely applying received ones.

## The Tell: Optimism vs. Pessimism

The characteristic feature of Fiat Progress: it is *gameable* as an optimism vs. pessimism debate. When you can ask "are you an optimist or a pessimist about progress?", you are operating in Fiat Progress mode.

The [[argument-of-progress|Argument of Progress]] cannot be structured this way — because x is expanding, the categories of good/bad must be reconstructed alongside the expanding models of reality.

## Political Power Technology

Optimism vs. pessimism is not just a legible frame for minds vulnerable to [[ontological-dread|Ontological Dread]] — it is a politically potent frame for the pursuit of power. "Promise an optimistic future, and a believable defense against pessimistic ones, and you gain power." This is why Fiat Progress was state-underwritten in the first place.

## Secularized Descendants

Most 20th-century ideologies of Progress were "merely secularized and globalized descendants of Fiat Progress stories." Even "radically modern-sounding grand narratives like those of Buckminster Fuller (Progress as ephemeralization — more and more for less and less) look like Fiat Progress stories once you learn to detect the characteristic features."

Contemporary examples of Fiat Progress in disguise:
- Whig history
- Leibnizean optimism ("best of all possible worlds")
- Collison-Cowen Progress Studies
- Thielean determinate optimism
- a16z-ish American Dynamism
- Any framework that structures Progress as a debate between optimists and pessimists

All of these treat x as constant and f as received — they just swap out "God's design" for "civilizational improvement" or "techno-optimism" as the fixed background.

## What Stripped It

Successive philosophers — [[baruch-spinoza|Spinoza]], [[david-hume|Hume]], and eventually the whole empiricist/naturalist tradition — abandoned the theological and political conceits underpinning Fiat Progress to focus purely on reality. The [[divergence-machine|Divergence Machine]] is what operates in the space left by Fiat Progress's dissolution.

## Residue and Displacement

The dissolution of Fiat Progress is not obviously liberating. Without it:
- No shared narrative of what counts as "better"
- No external authority to validate effort
- Increased divergence, but also increased [[ai-homogenization|averaging pressure]] from algorithmic systems that fill the vacuum

Covid + AI (2020-2025) made Fiat Progress narratives no longer tenable "with a straight face, even with billions of dollars and massive political operation behind it." This is one marker for the [[divergence-machine|Divergence Machine]] being fully turned on.
