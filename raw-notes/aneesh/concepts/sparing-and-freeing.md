# Sparing and Freeing

## Heidegger's Definition

> "To free really means to spare. The sparing itself consists not only in the fact that we do not harm the one whom we spare. Real sparing is something positive and takes place when we leave something beforehand in its own nature, when we return it specifically to its being, when we 'free' it in the real sense of the word into a preserve of peace."

From *Building Dwelling Thinking* (via [[Jan. 7, 2025: Building Dwelling Thinking]]).

Sparing is not merely non-harm — it is a positive act of *returning something to itself*, enabling it to be what it is.

## The Standard for Design

Applied to product and knowledge work:
- **Unkind placemaking**: products so convoluted that users must conform and constrict to use them
- **Kind placemaking**: products that preserve the user's nature and expand their capacity to explore spaces they care about

> "Everyone has their expertise and it is usually not in using your product."

The designer's expertise is the product. The user's expertise is the domain they're trying to navigate. Good design subordinates the first expertise to enable the second.

## Relation to Dwelling

Sparing is the "fundamental character of [[Dwelling as Philosophical Concept]]." To dwell in peace is to be spared — to exist within a "free sphere that safeguards each thing in its nature."

This reframes user experience: the question is not "can the user complete the task?" but "does the user's nature — their own expertise, curiosity, direction — remain intact and amplified through the interaction?"

## Comparison to Other Ethical Frameworks

| Framework | Freedom Means |
|-----------|--------------|
| Liberal (negative liberty) | Absence of coercion |
| Kantian autonomy | Rational self-legislation |
| Heidegger's sparing | Return to one's own nature |
| Manipulative design (antithesis) | Capture within the product's logic |

## Tension

The sparing ideal is in tension with commercial incentives in product design. Attention-maximizing products (see [[Personal Media vs Platform Media]]) are structurally opposed to sparing — they are designed to prevent return to one's own nature, to keep the user in the product's space.
