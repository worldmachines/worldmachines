# Dancing on the Shoulders of Giants

**Source**: [aneeshsathe.com, 2024-05-10](https://aneeshsathe.com/dancing-on-the-shoulders-of-giants/)
**Author**: [[Aneesh Sathe]]

## Central Argument

The frontier of expertise is not the accumulation of knowledge but the *structure* of knowledge networks and the agility to traverse them. LLMs solve the interaction problem but hallucinate; knowledge graphs are grounded and auditable but hard to query. Experts have taste and judgment but can only hold so much. The synthesis — Experts + LLMs + KGs — forms a composite material that could accelerate healthcare the way Wright's Law has accelerated semiconductors and solar energy: compounding gains from shared, externalized, traversable expertise.

## Key Claims

1. **Standing on shoulders is now baseline** — Newton's humility has become the minimum entry condition for professional expertise. The hard problems at the frontier require "muddling through" — risk-managed exploration between plausible options.
2. **Wright's Law applies to some industries, not others** — solar, semiconductors, and gene sequencing follow the cost-halving-per-doubling-output curve. Healthcare does not, because knowledge stays siloed in individual clinicians and only enters the broader system through generational education cycles.
3. **Hunter vs. dancer curiosity** — STEM favors tight, goal-directed conceptual clusters (hunter). Breakthroughs require high forward flow — conceptual leaps across distance (dancer). Feyerabend's "anything goes" as the epistemological correlate.
4. **Knowledge graphs mirror expert cognition** — KGs store entities, relationships, and metadata on relationships, analogous to how expert minds store knowledge relationally with context. PrimeKG combines multiple KGs for multimodal clinical reasoning. Retrieving from a KG is cited as 50x cheaper than LLM generation.
5. **LLMs solve KG's interactivity problem** — KGs are hard to query without technical expertise; LLMs provide natural language navigation. The combination: LLM handles interface, KG handles ground truth.
6. **The Giant's Causeway metaphor** — rather than slowly climbing accumulated knowledge through decades of experience, practitioners walk horizontally across externalized, structured expertise. Cognitive Task Analysis (from US military training) as a tool for extracting tacit expert knowledge into KG-compatible form.
